Walkaholic

AIMS

'Walking' is not easy to do because people do not know how long it takes to reach a proper amount of exercise.

This service, designed to address such concerns, works in conjunction with maps to calculate the location and distance users are currently walking.

TARGET AUDIENCE

It will be an app that help people who want to exercise steadily but don't have the will to do it, or people who exercise to maintain their health.

By competing with friends or others, Users can get motivation for exercising and check how much they have done a workout.

BRANDING

This logo is made by combining icons that represents a location and a walk.

VISUAL DIRECTION

The default colour used in the overall design is light blue.

This colour forms the main UI, and yellow is used as the point colour for the button or icon.

Since two colours are pretty vivid so light grey is also used to balance the design.

Since serif fonts have a conservative image, I have determined that the sans serif font is a good fit for Walkaholic.

Not Sans is the main font used for menu elements and Nanum Gothic is used for the content of the text.
